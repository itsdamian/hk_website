Bisyntax Builder

專為雙語學習者設計的句法意識培養工具，提升中英文句子結構理解和運用能力。

A syntactic awareness development tool designed specifically for bilingual learners to enhance understanding and application of Chinese and English sentence structures.

工具介紹

Tool Introduction

Bisyntax Builder 是結合科學研究證據開發的英語語法自學程式，適用於高小及初中學生，共有 20 個學習單元。學生可以在自學程式上上進行對比式學習，在每個單元中，學生需要完成不同的練習，包括句子結構分析、語法規則學習、多個語法主題，設有「Knowledge Bank」，可隨時回顧每個單元的重點知識，幫助學生鞏固多樣化的練習題，包括選擇題、句子重寫及選擇題，運用及鞏固語法知識。

Bisyntax Builder is an English grammar self-learning program developed based on scientific research evidence, suitable for upper primary and junior secondary students, with 20 learning units. Students can engage in comparative learning on the self-learning platform. In each unit, students need to complete different exercises, including sentence structure analysis, grammar rule learning, and multiple grammar topics. It features a "Knowledge Bank" that allows students to review key knowledge from each unit at any time, helping students consolidate diverse practice questions, including multiple choice, sentence rewriting, and selection questions, to apply and reinforce grammar knowledge.

研究背景與開發理念

Research Background and Development Philosophy

香港教育大學心理學系副教授徐秀博士及團隊致力於行為及認知神經科學面向的第二語言習得研究。基於多年的實證研究成果，團隊開發了 Bisyntax Builder 是一款自修博士及其團隊基於科學研究證據開發，專為學習英語語法第二語言的學生而設計的英語語法自學程式，運用人工智能，程式會根據學生的學習進度和能力，提供個人化的學習建議和練習。

Dr. Xu Xiu, Associate Professor in the Department of Psychology at The Education University of Hong Kong, and her team are dedicated to research on second language acquisition from behavioral and cognitive neuroscience perspectives. Based on years of empirical research findings, the team developed Bisyntax Builder, a self-learning English grammar program developed by Dr. Xu and her team based on scientific research evidence, specifically designed for students learning English grammar as a second language. Utilizing artificial intelligence, the program provides personalized learning recommendations and exercises based on students' learning progress and abilities.

適用對象

Target Users

Bisyntax Builder 專為高小及初中學生設計，特別適合以英語作為第二語言學習的學生。無論是希望提升英語語法基礎，還是需要系統性語法訓練的學習者，都能從這個科學化的學習工具中獲益。

Bisyntax Builder is specifically designed for upper primary and junior secondary students, particularly suitable for students learning English as a second language. Whether seeking to improve English grammar foundations or requiring systematic grammar training, learners can benefit from this scientifically-based learning tool.

科學研究基礎

Scientific Research Foundation

本程式基於香港教育大學心理學系的實證研究開發，結合行為科學和認知神經科學的最新發現，為第二語言習得提供科學化的學習方案。透過嚴謹的研究方法驗證，確保學習效果的可靠性和有效性。

This program is developed based on empirical research from the Department of Psychology at The Education University of Hong Kong, combining the latest findings in behavioral science and cognitive neuroscience to provide scientifically-based learning solutions for second language acquisition. Through rigorous research methodology validation, it ensures the reliability and effectiveness of learning outcomes.
