Back to School

幫助學生順利適應新學期，提供全面的學習支援和準備資源。

Helping students smoothly adapt to the new semester with comprehensive learning support and preparation resources.

應用程式概述

Application Overview

返校生活是由香港教育大學為從幼兒園到小學過渡的孩子開發的應用程式。此應用程式旨在提高孩子的英語能力以及他們學習英語的興趣和動力，為學童提供個人化的英語學習體驗。

Back to School is an application developed by The Education University of Hong Kong for children transitioning from kindergarten to primary school. This application aims to improve children's English abilities as well as their interest and motivation in learning English, providing students with a personalized English learning experience.

個人化學習評估

Personalized Learning Assessment

在應用程式的學習之旅之前，孩子們將首先完成英語能力評估。然後，該應用程式將根據孩子的表現生成個人化的學習序列，確保每個孩子都能獲得最適合自己程度的學習內容和進度安排。

Before the learning journey in the application, children will first complete an English proficiency assessment. The application will then generate a personalized learning sequence based on the child's performance, ensuring that each child receives learning content and progress arrangements most suitable for their level.

三大學習領域

Three Learning Areas

此應用程式包含3個學習領域，涵蓋英語學習的基礎知識和技能，為孩子建立穩固的英語基礎：

This application contains 3 learning areas that cover fundamental knowledge and skills in English learning, building a solid English foundation for children:

字母表學習

Alphabet Learning

系統性學習26個英文字母的認識、發音和書寫

Systematic learning of recognition, pronunciation, and writing of the 26 English letters

視覺詞訓練

Sight Words Training

培養孩子快速識別常用英文詞彙的能力

Develop children's ability to quickly recognize common English vocabulary

語音意識發展

Phonological Awareness Development

提升孩子對英語語音結構的理解和敏感度

Enhance children's understanding and sensitivity to English phonological structures

人工智能輔助學習

AI-Assisted Learning

該應用程式使用人工智能(AI)根據兒童的表現提供量身定制的反饋，讓孩子可以從自己的錯誤中更好地學習。AI系統會持續分析學習數據，調整教學策略，確保每個孩子都能在最適合的學習環境中進步。

The application uses artificial intelligence (AI) to provide tailored feedback based on children's performance, allowing children to learn better from their mistakes. The AI system continuously analyzes learning data and adjusts teaching strategies to ensure that each child can progress in the most suitable learning environment.

Learning Features and Advantages

  • Specifically designed for kindergarten-to-primary transition stage with strong targeting
  • 個人化學習路徑，因材施教
  • Personalized learning paths with individualized instruction
  • AI智能反饋，即時調整學習策略
  • AI intelligent feedback with real-time learning strategy adjustments
  • 涵蓋英語學習三大基礎領域
  • Covers three fundamental areas of English learning
  • 提升學習興趣和動力，培養積極學習態度
  • Enhance learning interest and motivation, cultivate positive learning attitudes

適用對象

Target Users

Back to School 應用程式專為從幼兒園過渡到小學的兒童設計，特別適合需要建立英語基礎的學前及小學低年級學生。透過科學化的評估和個人化的學習安排，幫助每個孩子順利適應小學英語學習環境。

The Back to School application is specifically designed for children transitioning from kindergarten to primary school, particularly suitable for preschool and lower primary students who need to establish English foundations. Through scientific assessment and personalized learning arrangements, it helps every child smoothly adapt to the primary school English learning environment.

教育研究支持

Educational Research Support

本應用程式由香港教育大學開發，基於最新的教育研究成果和兒童語言發展理論，結合人工智能技術，為幼小銜接階段的英語學習提供科學化、系統化的解決方案。

This application is developed by The Education University of Hong Kong, based on the latest educational research findings and child language development theories, combined with artificial intelligence technology to provide scientific and systematic solutions for English learning during the kindergarten-to-primary transition stage.
