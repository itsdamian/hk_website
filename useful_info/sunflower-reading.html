向日葵繪本閱讀計劃

Sunflower Picture Book Reading Program

向日葵繪本閱讀計劃

Sunflower Picture Book Reading Program

透過精選繪本培養兒童閱讀興趣，提升語言能力和想像力的創新閱讀計劃。

An innovative reading program that cultivates children's reading interest and enhances language skills and imagination through carefully selected picture books.

計劃概述

Program Overview

「向日葵繪本閱讀計劃」提升學生的識字能力、此「計劃」著重訓練幼兒閱讀中文識讀能力，為學生建立良好的讀書基礎，讓學生能夠有系統地學習中文識字，提升學生的語文能力。

The "Sunflower Picture Book Reading Program" enhances students' literacy skills. This "program" focuses on training young children's Chinese reading and recognition abilities, establishing a good reading foundation for students, enabling them to systematically learn Chinese character recognition and improve their language abilities.

教學方法與特色

Teaching Methods and Features

例如「水」字是個實質的部首，可組成雙字詞或多字詞，如「水果」、「水桶」、「自來水」和「水蒸氣」等詞彙，讓學生識字的同時，語言表達能力也能提升學生的讀寫能力和詞彙掌握。

For example, the character "水" (water) is a substantial radical that can form two-character or multi-character words, such as "水果" (fruit), "水桶" (bucket), "自來水" (tap water), and "水蒸氣" (water vapor). This allows students to improve their reading and writing abilities and vocabulary mastery while learning character recognition.

學習內容與範疇

Learning Content and Scope

教學內容包含各種基本詞彙及語言心理學的元素，讓學生認識閱讀概念，並運用於日常生活中，具體教學內容包括：認識自我、表達自我、建立正面自我概念等。

The teaching content includes various basic vocabulary and elements of psycholinguistics, helping students understand reading concepts and apply them in daily life. Specific teaching content includes: self-awareness, self-expression, and building positive self-concept.

教學活動設計

Teaching Activity Design

透過故事閱讀、情緒調節、理解情緒、問題心等，正面心理、愛、堅毅、勤奮、勇氣、可立自信、承擔精神等。

Through story reading, emotional regulation, understanding emotions, and problem-solving mindset, we cultivate positive psychology, love, perseverance, diligence, courage, self-confidence, and a sense of responsibility.

學習目標

Learning Objectives

識字能力提升

Literacy Enhancement

系統性學習中文字詞，建立扎實的識字基礎

Systematic learning of Chinese characters and words, establishing a solid literacy foundation

語言表達發展

Language Expression Development

透過詞彙學習提升讀寫能力和表達技巧

Enhance reading, writing abilities and expression skills through vocabulary learning

自我認知建立

Self-Awareness Building

培養學生認識自我、表達自我的能力

Cultivate students' ability to understand and express themselves

正面品格培養

Positive Character Development

透過故事培養愛心、堅毅、勇氣等正面品格

Develop positive character traits such as love, perseverance, and courage through stories

適合年齡與對象

Suitable Ages and Target Groups

  • 幼稚園學生：建立基礎識字能力
  • Kindergarten students: Building basic literacy skills
  • 小學低年級：強化中文閱讀理解
  • Lower primary students: Strengthening Chinese reading comprehension
  • 需要額外語文支援的學生
  • Students requiring additional language support
  • 希望提升中文能力的雙語學習者
  • Bilingual learners seeking to improve Chinese proficiency
