Reading Step by Step

系統性的閱讀能力培養計劃，從基礎到進階，逐步提升學生的閱讀理解能力。

A systematic reading ability development program that progressively enhances students' reading comprehension skills from basic to advanced levels.

平台概述

Platform Overview

閱讀步兵：詞綴篇由語言教育和研究常務委員會(SCOLAR)資助開發。該平台以英語詞綴和閱讀理解教學為主，專門設計來提升學生的英語詞彙理解能力和閱讀技巧，為學習者提供系統性的詞綴學習體驗。

Reading Step by Step: Affixes is funded and developed by the Standing Committee on Language Education and Research (SCOLAR). This platform focuses on English affixes and reading comprehension instruction, specifically designed to enhance students' English vocabulary comprehension abilities and reading skills, providing learners with a systematic affix learning experience.

學習模塊設計

Learning Module Design

該平台共有13個學習模塊，每個模塊涵蓋2-4個精選詞綴。這種結構化的設計確保學生能夠循序漸進地掌握英語詞綴的規律和應用，從基礎到進階系統性地建立詞彙知識。

The platform consists of 13 learning modules, with each module covering 2-4 selected affixes. This structured design ensures that students can progressively master the patterns and applications of English affixes, systematically building vocabulary knowledge from basic to advanced levels.

核心學習內容

Core Learning Content

英語詞綴教學

English Affixes Instruction

系統性學習前綴、後綴的意義和用法規則

Systematic learning of the meanings and usage rules of prefixes and suffixes

閱讀理解訓練

Reading Comprehension Training

結合詞綴知識提升文本理解和分析能力

Combine affix knowledge to enhance text comprehension and analysis abilities

詞彙擴展策略

Vocabulary Expansion Strategies

透過詞綴分析快速理解和記憶新詞彙

Quickly understand and memorize new vocabulary through affix analysis

自主學習體驗

Autonomous Learning Experience

學生可以在平台上的各種學習活動中享受自主學習。平台提供互動式的學習環境，讓學生能夠按照自己的學習節奏進行學習，透過多樣化的練習活動鞏固詞綴知識和閱讀技能。

Students can enjoy autonomous learning through various learning activities on the platform. The platform provides an interactive learning environment that allows students to learn at their own pace, consolidating affix knowledge and reading skills through diverse practice activities.

學習活動類型

Types of Learning Activities

  • 詞綴識別和分析練習
  • Affix identification and analysis exercises
  • 詞彙構成規律探索活動
  • Vocabulary formation pattern exploration activities
  • 閱讀理解綜合訓練
  • Comprehensive reading comprehension training
  • 互動式詞彙學習遊戲
  • Interactive vocabulary learning games
  • 自我評估和進度追蹤
  • Self-assessment and progress tracking

Deep understanding of English affix formation patterns and semantic functions

詞彙擴展

Vocabulary Expansion

運用詞綴知識快速學習和理解新詞彙

Use affix knowledge to quickly learn and understand new vocabulary

Reading Enhancement

Improve comprehension speed and accuracy of English texts

適用對象

Target Users

Reading Step by Step: 詞綴篇適合希望提升英語詞彙能力和閱讀理解技巧的學生，特別是中小學階段的英語學習者。透過系統性的詞綴學習，幫助學生建立有效的詞彙學習策略。

Reading Step by Step: Affixes is suitable for students who wish to enhance their English vocabulary abilities and reading comprehension skills, particularly English learners at the primary and secondary school levels. Through systematic affix learning, it helps students establish effective vocabulary learning strategies.

SCOLAR資助支持

SCOLAR Funding Support

本平台獲得語言教育和研究常務委員會(SCOLAR)的資助支持，確保教學內容的專業性和有效性。SCOLAR致力於推動香港的語言教育發展，為學習者提供優質的語言學習資源。

This platform is supported by funding from the Standing Committee on Language Education and Research (SCOLAR), ensuring the professionalism and effectiveness of the teaching content. SCOLAR is committed to promoting language education development in Hong Kong and providing quality language learning resources for learners.
