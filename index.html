第二語言學習及介入

Enhancing Children’s Language Learning through Training and Technology

我們的團隊旨在了解家庭環境、兒童閱讀發展（即語言覺察能力）的作用，以及科技或語言學習介入如何幫助學習者的第二語言學習。

Our team aims to understand the role of the family environment, children's reading development (i.e., language awareness), and how technological or language learning interventions can help learners with their second language acquisition.
