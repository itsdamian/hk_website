第二語言學習及介入

Enhancing Children’s Language Learning through Training and Technology

楊、林和唐三位學者在四個關鍵領域對兒童中文和英文的語言與讀寫能力發展產生了顯著影響： 提升學習者的中英文語言與讀寫能力：透過實施一系列基於實證的線上（plugged）和線下（unplugged）干預措施，這些干預不僅改變了課堂內外的教學實踐，還有效地提升了學生的中英文語言與讀寫技能。 指導家長在家中提供支援：透過與參與我們項目的家長進行密切溝通與合作，告知他們如何在家中以最佳實踐方式支援孩子的語言和讀寫能力發展。 改進中英文教學：為學校課程設計提供寶貴建議，從而改善中英文教學品質。 支援有閱讀困難的學生：透過多種方法，例如為特殊教育需求（SEN）學校的教師提供培訓課程，來支援有閱讀困難的學生。 將干預計劃商業化：特別是涉及社交機器人的項目，旨在創造一個對兒童友好的語言學習環境。 透過出版、培訓、媒體分享和工具商業化等多種途徑，這些影響力體現在促進兒童的發展以及改變教師和家長的實踐方式上。

YEUNG, LIN and TONG have generated significant impacts on children’s Chinese and English language and literacy development across four key areas: 1) enhancing Chinese and English language and literacy skills among learners by implementing a range of evidence-based plugged and unplugged interventions, which have transformed teaching practices both inside and outside the classroom; 2) informing parents about best practices for supporting language and literacy development at home through close communication and collaboration with parents involved in our projects; 3) improving Chinese and English instruction by providing valuable suggestions for the design of school curricula;4) supporting students with reading difficulties through various approaches, such as delivering training sessions for teachers in special education needs (SEN) schools;; and 5) commercializing our intervention programs, particularly those involving social robots, to foster a child-friendly language learning environment. Through a wide range of reach including publication, training, media sharing and commercialization of tools, impacts were manifested through enhanced development of children and changing practice of teachers and parents.
